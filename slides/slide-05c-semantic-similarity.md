# Semantic Similarity

## What Is Semantic Similarity?

**Academic Definition:**
> "Computationally, semantic similarity can be estimated by defining a topological similarity, by using ontologies to define the distance between terms/concepts"

### Breaking It Down:

**Topology:** Mathematical study of properties of space preserved under continuous deformations (stretching, twisting, but not tearing or gluing)

**Ontology:** Philosophical study of being - how we show properties of a subject area and how they are related

### Practical Translation:
- Measure how "close" concepts are in meaning
- Use mathematical space to represent conceptual relationships
- Similar meanings → Similar vectors → Close together in space
